1965 Corvette Stingray
“Pro-Street”

ENGINE
496 CI 4 bolt main, forged crank balanced & polished, JE pistons with 8 to 1 Compression.GM ported & polished Aluminum heads, Crane cams, hydraulic roller with .571lift, Crane roller rockers & stud girdle, BDS Blower with twin 750CFM Holley Carbs, Hooker Headers, 2.5” Flowmaster exhaust, MSD distributor & electronic ignition, Be Cool aluminum radiator with twin electric fans. Two Stewart water pumps one manual, second billet in line electric can be used while idling in traffic to keep operating temperature at a cool 180 degrees.  Performance 750 HP

TRANSMISSION
TCI built 4L80F full automatic transmission with 3500 Stahl converter Can be shifted manually has overdrive in 4th gear and Is fully controlled by on board programmable computer

REAREND
Ford 9 inch with Detroit Locker Posi unit with 4.10 Gears & Dutchman axles

SUSPENSION
Front has stainless steel tubular A-arms with coilovers.  Rear has 4 link with coilovers & a set of Morrison Wheelie Bars

BRAKES
Front & Rear brakes are 12” rotors w/4 piston caliper Willwood disc brakes.  Hurst line lock.  Lokar parking brake

WHEELS/TIRES
Weld Pro-Star Wheels Front-15x6”, Rear-15x15” Mickey Thompson Sportsman Tires, Front-28x7.50x15, Rear- 31x18.50x15

FRAME
Art Morrison 2x4 square tube custom chassis with all steel floor and wheel tubs all powder coated gloss black. 
Full 10 point cage, rack & pinion steering.  All chassis fabrication was completed by Bragg Automotive

STYLE
Body is completely original 1965 Convertible with minor modifications.  Lengthened rear fenders, hood cut out for blower and deleted front bumpers & wipers. The paint is DuPont (Corvette Torch Red) base coat – clear coat.  Several coats of base and clear coat were applied with hand sanding between each coat after which the entire body was hand sanded and buffed with professional buffers.  Body was removed from chassis to powder coat chassis and prep body before painting. 

INTERIOR
Monaco seats are black with red inserts & red piping.  Both seats have RCI 5 Point Harnesses.  The neatest part of the interior is the Kenwood Stereo CD player, which mechanically lifts out of the dash speaker cover.  Autometer gauges are mounted on a custom gauge pod.  A custom console was built to accommodate the shifter.  Custom made door panels are covered with black and red leather.  The center dash panel is covered with black leather and sports a 1962 Corvette emblem.  GM tilt steering column with LeCarra aluminum steering wheel. 

ELECTRICAL
The car was completely rewired with an American Auto Wire System with all modern fuses & relays.  The system uses 2 trunk mounted Optima batteries.

FUEL
A truck mounted 12 gallon Triangle Engineering custom made aluminum fuel cell, foam filled with 12/volt sender. Fuel flows through twin Holley pumps through a ½ inch fuel line with Holley fuel regulator.
